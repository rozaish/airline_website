Frequently Asked Questions

What is the baggage allowance?

Economy passengers can carry 1 checked bag (23kg) and 1 cabin bag (7kg). Business and First Class passengers have higher allowances.


Can I change my booking?



Yes, bookings can be modified up to 24 hours before departure. Fees may apply depending on your ticket type.


Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are available for refundable tickets. Non-refundable tickets may be eligible for travel credit.
